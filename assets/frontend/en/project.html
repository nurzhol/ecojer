ECO-OFFICE

Today, over 100 million tonnes of municipal waste has accumulated in Kazakhstan's landfills. Annually, that figure is increasing by 4m tonnes.
Most landfills have already reached capacity, andnew land plots will be necessary for waste disposal.
This problem has a solution.
The EcoJer Association, together with EcoNetwork, proposes to actively develop the sorting waste culture.
We are going to show by example that this is easy and possible.
In the offices of the Association, we install special eco binsto separate waste for further processing.
These eco bins will be installed in the building, and EcoNetwork specialists will explain to employees the way to use the ecobinsand sort waste properly.
If each citizen of Kazakhstan starts sorting waste, only 10-15% will remain in landfills, and the rest of the waste will be recycled without harming the environment.
We encourage all companies and offices to support our initiative with our partners.
Install eco bins in your offices and contribute to improving the environment!
